News

Regular updates from our groups.

  • November 13, 2023 -- PhD student Valentin Knappich has participated in the PLABA shared task on simplifying medical text and achieved excellent performance scores both in the automatic and the manual evaluation. Congrats, Valentin! Read more here.
  • October 25, 2023 -- Our paper SciOL and MuLMS-Img: Introducing A Large-Scale Multimodal Scientific Dataset and Models for Image-Text Tasks in the Scientific Domain has been accepted to WACV 2024. The paper describes new datasets and models for language-vision tasks in the scientific domain. This is joint work with the Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence.
  • October 10, 2023 -- Our paper Is the Answer in the Text? Challenging ChatGPT with Evidence Retrieval from Instructive Text has been accepted to the Findings of EMNLP 2023. Congrats to the team, Sophie, Talita, and Wei! Our paper MuLMS: A Multi-Layer Annotated Text Corpus for Information Extraction in the Materials Science Domain has been accepted to The 2nd Workshop on Information Extraction from Scientific Publications which will take place at IJCNLP-AACL 2023. Congrats, Timo, Matteo, and Stefan!
  • October 4-5, 2023 -- Our Human Language Technology @ Augsburg Kickoff Workshop took place at Augsburg University.
  • July 2023 -- Paper on Argumentative Zoning in the materials science domain accepted to CODI@ ACL 2023. Congrats, Timo and Sophie!
