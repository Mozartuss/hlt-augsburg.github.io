News

Regular updates from our groups.

  • March 15, 2024 -- NAACL main conference paper accepted: FREB-TQA: A Fine-Grained Robustness Evaluation Benchmark for Table Question Answering. Congrats, Wei! The paper is joint work with Mohsen Mesgar (Bosch) and Heike Adel (Hochschule der Medien, Stuttgart).
  • February 21, 2024 -- Our paper AnnoCTR: A Dataset for Detecting and Linking Entities, Tactics, and Techniques in Cyber Threat Reports has been accepted to LREC/Coling, the 2024 Joint International Conference on Computational Linguistics, Language Resources, and Evaluation, which will take place in May in Torino, Italy. In the paper, which is a collaboration with Bosch and the MPI of Informatics in Saarbrücken, we present a new dataset of classifying cyber attacks and techniques in reports. Also, the paper Explaining Pre-Trained Language Models with Attribution Scores: An Analysis in Low-Resource Settings by our Phd student Wei Zhou, together with Bosch and Univerisity of Stuttgart, got accepted at LREC-COLING 2024
  • February 8, 2024 -- Annemarie Friedrich has been elected as President of the German Society for Computational Linguistics and Language Technology (GSCL).
  • January 24, 2024 -- Our paper Aligning Uncertainty: Leveraging LLMs to Analyze Uncertainty Transfer in Text Summarization has been accepted to the UncertaiNLP Workshop at EACL 2024. Congrats, Zahra!
  • January 17, 2024 -- Annemarie Friedrich is giving a lecture on Ethics and NLP in the Ringvorlesung Alexa, ChatGPT & Co: Wie haltet ihr es mit der Ethik? - Eine interdisziplinäre Perspektive auf KI.
